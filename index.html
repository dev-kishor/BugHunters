About Us


BugHunters Software Solution works as your IT department. We provide web designing, web development, database integration and onsite computer support to small to medium companies. We have highly qualified and experienced teams of web designers, programmers and network engineers who can assist you in revamping your website or setup your network without costing you a fortune.


Being a team of qualified Web Professionals and Business Consultants, we are geared to take up projects of any size and complexity. With our strong focus on business value of all technology development projects, we make sure that the focus stays on how best we can use the freshest technology for serving business needs and not the other way around.

We invite you to contact us for more details or for initiating discussions on how we can help you become more profitable, by taking care of all your Web problems with professional competence and ease. You can visit us at our panipat office and have a personal discussion over how to go about your website requirement.
